Funded Trader

Funded trading, also known as proprietary (prop) trading, involves trading financial instruments using capital provided by a firm rather than personal funds. In this arrangement, traders are given access to the firm's capital and resources to trade, and in return, they share a portion of the profits with the firm.

Consistency

Consistency in trading is a crucial attribute, especially in the context of funded trading, where traders are using capital provided by proprietary firms. Consistency is not just about making profits but maintaining steady performance and adhering to risk management rules.
