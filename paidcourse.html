HTF Bias (Higher Time Frame Bias) helps traders to understand the dominant trend in higher time frames, which can provide a better context for making trading decisions in lower time frames.

To look for trades:: 1. Monthly Bias:::::weekly rejection +Transfer of order on weekly zone (Example:for sell ::: first buy and then sell candles formation & for buy ::: first sell and then buy candles formation)

2. Weekly Bias:::::Daily rejection +Transfer of order on daily zone

3. Daily Bias :::::Daily rejection +Transfer of order on 4hr zone(for sell : first buy and then sell candles formation)

4. Daily is the lowest time frame we go for storyline and H4 & H1 is the lowest we go for entry

5. Left Shoulder and Apex are the main two areas to trade

6. Open close candles (OCC) are also valid for trade and it is more powerful when aligned with the engulfing candle

7. Always look for fresh scenario to trade

Note: First candle rejection is called Left Shoulder and Candle top is denoted by Apex.We can take after these area was mitigated.If these all requirements filled up then we look for sessions liquidity and lower time frame confirmation.

RSI-Divergence is a trading strategy that uses the Relative Strength Index (RSI) to identify potential reversals in the market. Divergence occurs when the price moves in the opposite direction of the RSI, indicating a possible change in trend.
